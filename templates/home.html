About DIT University

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1998, DIT University has grown to become a premier institution for higher education in India. With a commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development, we have been shaping the leaders of tomorrow for over two decades.

  • NAAC 'A+' Grade Accreditation
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • Ranked among Top 100 Universities in India
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities
  • Industry partnerships with leading global companies

Our Journey

1998

Founded as Dehradun Institute of Technology with a vision to provide quality technical education.

2009

Expanded academic offerings to include management and science programs.

2012

Granted university status and renamed as DIT University.

2017

Received NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation for academic excellence.

2023

Upgraded to NAAC 'A+' Grade and ranked among the top 100 universities in India.
