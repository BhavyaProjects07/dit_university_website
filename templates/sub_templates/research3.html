Research Overview

Project Description

Our research explores the application of blockchain technology to enhance transparency, traceability, and security in global supply chain networks. Traditional supply chains often suffer from opacity, inefficiency, and vulnerability to fraud, which can lead to significant economic losses and reputational damage.

By leveraging blockchain's immutable and distributed ledger technology, we're developing innovative solutions that provide end-to-end visibility across complex supply chains, enable real-time tracking of goods, verify product authenticity, and streamline documentation processes. Our goal is to create more resilient, efficient, and trustworthy supply chain ecosystems that benefit all stakeholders.

Smart Contracts

Creating automated smart contracts that execute predefined actions when specific conditions are met, reducing paperwork and streamlining transactions.

Security & Anti-Counterfeiting

Implementing blockchain solutions to combat counterfeiting and ensure product authenticity through cryptographic verification and immutable records.

Research Methodology

  1. Supply Chain Analysis: Studying existing supply chain processes across various industries to identify pain points and opportunities for blockchain integration.
  2. Blockchain Architecture Design: Developing customized blockchain architectures tailored to specific supply chain requirements, considering factors like scalability, privacy, and consensus mechanisms.
  3. Smart Contract Development: Creating and testing smart contracts that automate key supply chain processes and enforce business rules.
  4. Prototype Implementation: Building working prototypes and proof-of-concept systems to validate blockchain solutions in real-world supply chain scenarios.
  5. Performance Evaluation: Assessing the performance, security, and economic impact of blockchain implementations compared to traditional systems.

Current Projects

Farm-to-Table Traceability Platform

Developing a blockchain-based platform that tracks agricultural products from farm to consumer, providing complete transparency about origin, farming practices, and handling throughout the supply chain.

2022-2025 Lead: Dr. Sunita Patel

Pharmaceutical Supply Chain Security

Creating a blockchain solution for the pharmaceutical industry to combat counterfeit medications, ensure proper handling conditions, and streamline regulatory compliance.

2023-2026 Lead: Dr. Anand Kumar

Cross-Border Trade Documentation

Developing a blockchain platform that digitizes and automates cross-border trade documentation, reducing paperwork, eliminating errors, and accelerating customs clearance processes.

2021-2024 Lead: Dr. Sunita Patel, Dr. Anand Kumar
