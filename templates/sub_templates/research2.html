Research Overview

Project Description

Our Renewable Energy and Integration Systems research program at DIT University is dedicated to developing innovative technologies and methodologies for harnessing renewable energy sources and integrating them effectively into existing power infrastructure. As the world transitions toward sustainable energy systems, our work addresses the critical challenges of reliability, efficiency, and scalability in renewable energy deployment.

We focus on a comprehensive approach that spans the entire renewable energy ecosystemâ€”from advanced solar and wind energy capture technologies to smart grid integration, energy storage solutions, and demand-side management. Our multidisciplinary team combines expertise in electrical engineering, materials science, computer science, and environmental engineering to create holistic solutions for the energy challenges of the 21st century.

Through partnerships with industry leaders, government agencies, and international research institutions, we're working to accelerate the global energy transition by making renewable energy systems more accessible, affordable, and reliable for communities across India and beyond.

Research Methodology

  1. Materials Research & Development: Investigating novel materials and structures for improved energy capture, conversion, and storage, with a focus on sustainability and scalability.
  2. System Modeling & Simulation: Creating comprehensive models of renewable energy systems and their integration with existing infrastructure to predict performance and optimize designs.
  3. Prototype Development & Testing: Building and testing scaled prototypes of renewable energy technologies and integration systems under controlled and real-world conditions.
  4. Field Deployment & Monitoring: Implementing pilot projects in diverse settings to gather operational data and validate performance in actual usage scenarios.
  5. Techno-Economic Analysis: Conducting comprehensive assessments of technological, economic, and environmental factors to ensure practical viability and market relevance.

Current Projects

High-Efficiency Perovskite-Silicon Tandem Solar Cells

Developing tandem solar cell technology that combines perovskite and silicon layers to achieve higher efficiency than conventional silicon cells while maintaining long-term stability and cost-effectiveness.

2022-2025 Lead: Dr. Sunita Patel

Grid-Scale Vanadium Redox Flow Battery System

Designing and optimizing large-scale vanadium redox flow battery systems for grid energy storage, with a focus on improving energy density, cycle life, and reducing manufacturing costs.

2023-2026 Lead: Dr. Rajesh Verma

AI-Powered Microgrid Management System

Developing an intelligent control system that uses machine learning to optimize the operation of microgrids with high renewable penetration, balancing generation, storage, and demand in real-time.

2021-2024 Lead: Dr. Anand Kumar, Dr. Sunita Patel
