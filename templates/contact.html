Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, we require a strong academic record, entrance exam scores, and sometimes an interview. Visit our Admissions Requirements page for detailed information.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, DIT University offers various merit-based and need-based scholarships. Check our Scholarships page for eligibility criteria and application process.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

We welcome prospective students and families to visit our campus. You can schedule a tour by filling out the form on our Campus Visit page or by calling our admissions office.

What accommodation options are available?

DIT University provides on-campus hostel facilities for both boys and girls with modern amenities. Learn more about our accommodation options on the Accommodation page.

Getting Here

By Air

Jolly Grant Airport (DED) is approximately 30 km from the campus. Taxis are available from the airport.

By Train

Dehradun Railway Station is about 10 km from the campus. Auto-rickshaws and taxis are readily available.

By Bus

Regular bus services connect Dehradun to major cities. The ISBT is about 8 km from the campus.
