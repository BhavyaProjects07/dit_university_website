Admission Process

Join DIT University in a few simple steps. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and student-friendly.

1

Online Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details.

Required Documents:

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Entrance Exam Score Card (if applicable)
  • ID Proof
2

Entrance Exam / Interview

Appear for the university entrance exam or interview as per your program requirements.

We accept scores from national entrance exams like JEE, NEET, CAT, MAT, etc. depending on the program.

3

Offer Letter & Fee Payment

Receive your offer letter and complete the admission by paying the initial fee.

Multiple payment options available including EMI facilities and scholarships for meritorious students.

4

Enrollment & Orientation

Complete the enrollment process and attend the orientation program.

Our comprehensive orientation program helps you get familiar with the campus, faculty, and fellow students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions, programs, and campus life.

Eligibility criteria vary by program. Generally, for undergraduate programs, you need to have completed 10+2 with minimum 50-60% marks in relevant subjects. For postgraduate programs, a bachelor's degree in the relevant field with minimum 50-60% marks is required. Some programs may also require valid entrance exam scores.

Yes, DIT University offers various merit-based and need-based scholarships. These include scholarships for academic excellence, sports achievements, and for students from economically weaker sections. Details about specific scholarship programs and eligibility criteria can be found on our scholarships page.

DIT University has an excellent placement record with over 95% placement for eligible students. Our students are placed in top companies across various sectors with competitive salary packages. The university has a dedicated placement cell that works year-round to provide career opportunities to students.

Yes, DIT University provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls. The hostels are equipped with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, recreational areas, gym, and dining facilities. Hostel accommodation is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, so early application is recommended.
